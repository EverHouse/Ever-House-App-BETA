2. **`SimulatorAdmin`**:
* Refactored the layout into a responsive grid.
* **Desktop:** Shows Queue (Left) and Calendar (Right) side-by-side.
* **Mobile:** Keeps the tab switcher to toggle views.
* Enabled independent scrolling for the panels on desktop so a long queue doesn't push the calendar off-screen.
